Frequently Asked Questions

Do piano moving services include tuning the piano after the move?

Most piano moving services do not include tuning the piano after the move. Tuning is a specialized skill that requires a trained professional. However, some moving companies may offer tuning services for an additional fee or can recommend a piano tuner for you to hire separately. It is important to let the piano settle for a few weeks after the move before tuning it, as the strings and other components may have shifted during transportation. This will ensure that the piano is properly tuned and ready to be played. It is recommended to schedule a tuning appointment with a professional piano tuner after the move to ensure that your piano sounds its best.

Are there any weight restrictions for piano moving services?

Yes, there are weight restrictions for piano moving services. The weight restrictions can vary depending on the type and size of the piano being moved. Generally, upright pianos can weigh between 300 to 800 pounds, while grand pianos can weigh between 500 to 1200 pounds. It is important for piano moving services to have the proper equipment and trained professionals to safely move heavy pianos. Some moving companies may have specific weight limits for their services, so it is recommended to inquire about this before scheduling a move. Additionally, factors such as stairs, narrow doorways, and distance to be moved can also impact the ability to move a heavy piano safely. Overall, it is essential to work with a reputable and experienced piano moving company to ensure that the piano is handled and transported with care and expertise.

Do piano moving services offer international moving options?

Yes, there are piano moving services that offer international moving options. These services specialize in safely transporting pianos across borders and overseas to ensure they arrive at their destination in perfect condition. They have the expertise and equipment to handle the unique challenges of international piano moving, such as navigating customs regulations, securing proper documentation, and coordinating logistics for transportation by air, sea, or land. International piano moving services may also offer additional services such as piano tuning and climate-controlled storage to protect the instrument during transit. It is important to research and choose a reputable piano moving company with experience in international moves to ensure a smooth and successful relocation of your piano to its new home.
